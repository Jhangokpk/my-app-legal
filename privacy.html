Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

TKAAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our autonomous TikTok posting service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the Service:

  • TikTok Account Access Token — granted by you via TikTok's OAuth flow, used to post content on your behalf.
  • TikTok User ID — used to identify your account within our system.
  • Posting Activity Logs — records of posts made through TKAAI, used for debugging and audit purposes.

We do not collect your TikTok password. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment details unless you explicitly provide them.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Authenticate and post content to TikTok on your behalf
  • Maintain your posting schedule and history
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Comply with TikTok's API requirements and policies

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

4. TikTok Data

TKAAI accesses your TikTok account through TikTok's official API. Any data retrieved from TikTok is used exclusively to fulfill the posting and scheduling functions of the Service. We handle TikTok user data in compliance with TikTok's API Terms of Service and Developer Policies.

We do not sell, rent, or share your TikTok data with third parties.

5. Data Retention

We retain your access token and activity logs for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your access token will be revoked and all associated data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

TKAAI integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for TikTok's data practices.

8. International Users

TKAAI is available worldwide. By using the Service, you consent to the processing of your data in the country where our servers are hosted. We take appropriate steps to ensure your data is treated in accordance with this Privacy Policy regardless of where it is processed.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@tkaai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

TKAAI is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the Service, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Us
